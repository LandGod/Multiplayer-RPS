Instructions

To start the game, give your name, then press "play." After connecting with an opponent, you'll have 15 seconds to click either 'rock,' 'paper,' or 'scissors.' If you can't choose within that ammount of time you lose. Otherwise, after the other play has also made a choice, you will find out if you won, lost, or had a draw. After that you can have a rematch if you like, provided both you and your opponet click the rematch button. Or, you can quit. It's all up to you; the world is your oyster.
